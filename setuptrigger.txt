Set up the trigger:
Click "Save" (disk icon)
Click "Triggers" (clock icon in left sidebar)
Click "Add Trigger" (bottom right)
For "Choose which function to run", select "onFormSubmit"
For "Select event source", choose "From spreadsheet"
For "Select event type", choose "On form submit"
Click "Save"
Authorize the script:
You'll need to grant permission to the script to send emails
Follow the prompts to authorize

The primary email (alfredpapa1422@gmail.com) will receive the notification as the main recipient
The other two emails (alfredpapa141406@gmail.com and ragnarok0q@gmail.com) will be CC'd
All three accounts will receive the same detailed notification with the booking information
