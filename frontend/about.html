About Our Transient House

Learn more about our story and values

Our Story

Founded in 2015, our Transient House started as a small family-owned accommodation with just 3 rooms. What began as a simple dream to provide comfortable lodging for travelers has now grown into a beloved destination for both tourists and locals alike.

Over the years, we've expanded our facilities while maintaining the warm, personalized service that has become our signature. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that every guest feels at home during their stay with us.

Located in a charming neighborhood with easy access to local attractions, our Transient House offers the perfect balance of comfort, convenience, and value. Whether you're visiting for business or pleasure, our goal is to make your stay memorable and stress-free.

Our Values

These core principles guide everything we do

Comfort

We believe that a good night's sleep is essential for a great experience. That's why we invest in quality mattresses, linens, and amenities to ensure your utmost comfort.

Hospitality

We treat every guest like family. Our friendly staff is always ready to assist you with a smile, making sure your needs are met promptly and courteously.

Safety

Your safety is our priority. We maintain strict security protocols and regularly update our facilities to ensure a secure environment for all our guests.

Cleanliness

We maintain the highest standards of cleanliness in all our rooms and common areas. Regular sanitization ensures a hygienic environment for your peace of mind.
